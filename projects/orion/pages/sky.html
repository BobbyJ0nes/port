Scene III

Some wispy clouds make their way across the open sky. Tilting his head slightly, he followed the one shaped like a triceratops on its journey, until it morphs into a shape more similar to one of those Aston Martin's from a Sean Connery Bond film.

The stars blinked brighter now that his focus was on their canvas, one even shot across it.
